# Competitive Monitoring Cadence

Guide for what to track, how often, and which tools to use.

## Monitoring Framework

### Priority Tiers

| Priority | Examples | Frequency | Response Time |
|----------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Critical** | Pricing changes, product launches, M&A, major announcements | Real-time/Daily | Immediate alert |
| **Important** | Marketing campaigns, content strategy, hiring patterns | Weekly | Weekly digest |
| **Contextual** | Industry trends, analyst reports, patent filings | Monthly/Quarterly | Monthly review |

---

## What to Track

### Critical (Real-Time/Daily)

**Pricing Changes**
- Public pricing page updates
- Promotional offers
- Packaging/tier changes
- Contract term changes

**Product Releases**
- New features announced
- Product launches
- Major updates/versions
- Deprecations/EOL announcements

**Business News**
- Funding rounds
- Acquisitions/mergers
- Executive changes
- Major partnerships
- Layoffs/restructuring

**Key Indicators:**
| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Price drop | Aggressive growth push or market pressure | Review our pricing competitiveness |
| New feature | Addressing gap we have, or differentiation | Assess if it affects our positioning |
| New funding | Will likely invest in growth/marketing | Prepare for increased competition |
| Leadership change | Potential strategy shift | Watch for messaging/product changes |

### Important (Weekly)

**Marketing & Messaging**
- Homepage messaging changes
- Value proposition updates
- Competitive positioning shifts
- Campaign themes

**Content Strategy**
- Blog post topics and frequency
- Webinars and events
- Case studies published
- SEO keywords targeted

**Social & Community**
- Social media activity
- Community engagement
- Influencer partnerships
- Thought leadership positioning

**Customer Signals**
- G2/Capterra reviews
- Glassdoor reviews
- Social mentions
- Support forum activity

**Key Indicators:**
| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Messaging pivot | New positioning, possibly new target market | Update battle cards |
| Content surge | Push into new topic/keyword territory | SEO response |
| Negative reviews spike | Product/service issues | Sales enablement opportunity |
| Positive reviews surge | Customer success investment | Competitive response needed |

### Contextual (Monthly/Quarterly)

**Hiring Patterns**
- Engineering team growth (product investment)
- Sales team growth (go-to-market push)
- New role types (market expansion signals)
- Geographic expansion

**Strategic Signals**
- Patent filings
- Trademark applications
- Domain registrations
- Partnership announcements

**Market Position**
- Analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester)
- Market share data
- Industry rankings
- Conference presence

**Key Indicators:**
| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Engineering hiring surge | Major product investment coming | Track roadmap signals |
| New market jobs | Geographic/vertical expansion | Assess competitive threat |
| Patent filing | Technology differentiation | Analyze IP implications |
| Analyst recognition | Third-party validation | Counter-positioning needed |

## Monitoring Tools & Methods

### Automated Monitoring

**Website Change Detection**
| Tool | Use Case | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| Visualping | Page change alerts | Free tier available |
| Wachete | Multi-page monitoring | Paid |
| ChangeTower | Enterprise monitoring | Paid |

**Setup Priority Pages:**
- Pricing page
- Product/features page
- Homepage
- Blog/news section
- Job board

**News & Alerts**
| Tool | Use Case | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| Google Alerts | Free monitoring | Free |
| Feedly | RSS aggregation | Free/Paid |
| Meltwater | Enterprise news | Paid |
| Mention | Social + web | Paid |

**Alert Keywords to Set:**
- "[Competitor name]"
- "[Competitor name] funding"
- "[Competitor name] acquisition"
- "[Competitor name] vs [your company]"
- "[Competitor name] review"

**SEO & Traffic**
| Tool | Use Case | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| SEMrush | Keyword tracking | Paid |
| Ahrefs | Backlink monitoring | Paid |
| Similarweb | Traffic estimates | Free/Paid |

**Track:**
- Organic traffic trends
- Paid search keywords
- Backlink growth
- Content publication rate

### Manual Monitoring

**Weekly Review Checklist:**
- [ ] Visit each competitor homepage
- [ ] Check pricing pages for changes
- [ ] Review recent blog posts
- [ ] Scan social media activity
- [ ] Check G2/Capterra for new reviews
- [ ] Review job postings

**Monthly Deep Dive:**
- [ ] Product demo/trial (if available)
- [ ] Competitor webinar attendance
- [ ] Analyst report review
- [ ] Win/loss data analysis
- [ ] Sales team debrief

## Best Practices

### Do
- Automate wherever possible
- Create clear escalation paths
- Tag and categorize all intelligence
- Share insights proactively (don't wait to be asked)
- Build relationships with sales for frontline intel

### Don't
- Overwhelm with information (filter for relevance)
- Monitor without action (every insight should drive a response)
- Rely solely on automated tools (human analysis matters)
- Ignore indirect competitors
- Let monitoring become sporadic
