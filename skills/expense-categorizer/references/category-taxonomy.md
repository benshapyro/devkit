# Expense Category Taxonomy

Complete list of expense categories with GL codes and tax treatment.

## Category Structure

```
[Category]
└── [Subcategory]
    ├── GL Code: [XXXX-XX]
    ├── Tax Rate: [X%]
    └── Common Examples: [List]
```

---

## Travel (6100)

### Airfare (6100-01)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Flights, baggage fees, seat upgrades, change fees
- **Requirements:** Business purpose, destination, dates
- **Policy Notes:** Economy class standard; business class requires approval

### Lodging (6100-02)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Hotels, motels, Airbnb, extended stay
- **Requirements:** Business purpose, nightly rate, dates
- **Policy Notes:** Reasonable rate for location; receipt always required

### Ground Transportation (6100-03)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Taxi, Uber, Lyft, car service, airport shuttles
- **Requirements:** Origin, destination, business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Most economical option appropriate for situation

### Car Rental (6100-04)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Rental cars, fuel for rentals, tolls
- **Requirements:** Business purpose, rental period
- **Policy Notes:** Intermediate size standard; upgrade requires approval

### Mileage Reimbursement (6100-05)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Personal vehicle for business use
- **Requirements:** Starting point, destination, miles, purpose
- **Rate:** 67 cents/mile (2026 IRS rate)
- **Policy Notes:** Log all trips; submit monthly

### Parking (6100-06)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Parking lots, meters, valet (business travel)
- **Requirements:** Location, business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Reasonable for location

### Travel Meals (6100-07)
- **Tax Rate:** 50%
- **Examples:** Meals while traveling overnight
- **Requirements:** Away from tax home, business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Per diem may apply; keep receipts

### Other Travel (6100-99)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Tips, phone calls, laundry during extended travel
- **Requirements:** Business necessity documented

## Meals & Entertainment (6200)

### Client Meals (6200-01)
- **Tax Rate:** 50%
- **Examples:** Business meals with clients, prospects, partners
- **Requirements:** Attendees, business purpose, relationship
- **Policy Notes:** Limit per person; pre-approval for large groups

### Team Meals (6200-02)
- **Tax Rate:** 50%
- **Examples:** Team lunches, department meals, celebrations
- **Requirements:** Attendees, business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Must have business discussion; not purely social

### Coffee/Snacks (6200-03)
- **Tax Rate:** 50%
- **Examples:** Coffee meetings, working sessions with refreshments
- **Requirements:** Business purpose, attendees if applicable
- **Policy Notes:** Reasonable amounts

### Working Meals (6200-04)
- **Tax Rate:** 50%
- **Examples:** Lunch during required meetings, overtime meals
- **Requirements:** Business necessity
- **Policy Notes:** Document why meal was business-required

### Entertainment (6200-10)
- **Tax Rate:** 0%
- **Examples:** Tickets, sporting events, concerts, golf
- **Requirements:** Attendees, business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Generally NOT deductible after 2017 tax law
- **Warning:** Review carefully; most entertainment is non-deductible

## Office Expenses (6300)

### Office Supplies (6300-01)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Paper, pens, staplers, folders, desk accessories
- **Requirements:** Business use
- **Policy Notes:** Reasonable quantities

### Postage & Shipping (6300-02)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** USPS, FedEx, UPS, courier services
- **Requirements:** Business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Use company accounts when available

### Printing & Copying (6300-03)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Print shop services, copies, business cards
- **Requirements:** Business use
- **Policy Notes:** Consider in-house alternatives

### Furniture & Fixtures (6300-04)
- **Tax Rate:** 100% (under $2,500) or Depreciate
- **Examples:** Desks, chairs, shelving, lamps
- **Requirements:** Business use, approval for large purchases
- **Policy Notes:** Items over $2,500 may require capitalization

### Office Equipment (6300-05)
- **Tax Rate:** 100% (under $2,500) or Depreciate
- **Examples:** Printers, shredders, coffee makers
- **Requirements:** Business use
- **Policy Notes:** Consider lease vs. buy for expensive items

### Cleaning & Maintenance (6300-06)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Cleaning supplies, maintenance services
- **Requirements:** Business premises
- **Policy Notes:** Regular business expense

## Technology (6400)

### Software Subscriptions (6400-01)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** SaaS tools, cloud services, app subscriptions
- **Requirements:** Business use, approval for new subscriptions
- **Policy Notes:** Annual payments may require prorating

### Software Licenses (6400-02)
- **Tax Rate:** 100% or Amortize
- **Examples:** Perpetual licenses, enterprise software
- **Requirements:** Business use, IT approval
- **Policy Notes:** Large licenses may require amortization

### Hardware (6400-03)
- **Tax Rate:** 100% (under $2,500) or Depreciate
- **Examples:** Computers, monitors, keyboards, mice, cables
- **Requirements:** Business use, IT approval for large purchases
- **Policy Notes:** De minimis safe harbor applies under $2,500

### Mobile Devices (6400-04)
- **Tax Rate:** 100% (under $2,500) or Depreciate
- **Examples:** Phones, tablets, accessories
- **Requirements:** Business use percentage
- **Policy Notes:** Personal use portion may not be deductible

### Cloud Services (6400-05)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, hosting
- **Requirements:** Business use
- **Policy Notes:** Track by project/department

### IT Services (6400-06)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** IT support, consulting, implementation
- **Requirements:** Business purpose
- **Policy Notes:** Large engagements may require PO

### Telecommunications (6400-07)
- **Tax Rate:** 100%
- **Examples:** Phone service, internet, video conferencing
- **Requirements:** Business use
- **Policy Notes:** Allocate personal vs. business use
