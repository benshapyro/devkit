## Best Practices

### Calendar Maintenance

1. **Review weekly:** Update statuses, catch slippage early
2. **Plan monthly:** Detail next month while current month executes
3. **Adjust quarterly:** Revisit pillars and goals based on performance

### Status Discipline

Use consistent status labels:
- **Planned** → **Brief** → **Draft** → **Review** → **Final** → **Scheduled** → **Published**

### Realistic Capacity

Calculate production capacity honestly:
- Writer: X posts/week at Y quality level
- Editor: X reviews/day
- Designer: X assets/week

Plan at 80% capacity for flexibility.

### Buffer Content

Maintain 2-4 "evergreen" pieces ready to publish:
- Covers unexpected gaps
- Reduces publishing pressure
- Works for any time of year
