## 2. Assessment Methodology

### Framework Overview
This assessment evaluates AI transformation maturity using a four-phase framework based on:
- OpenAI's deployment best practices
- McKinsey State of AI research
- Stanford HAI AI Index
- Gartner enterprise AI benchmarks

### Assessment Process
1. **Structured Interviews**: [XX] stakeholders across [XX] departments
2. **Documentation Review**: [List key documents reviewed]
3. **Evidence Validation**: [Artifacts examined]
4. **Benchmark Analysis**: Comparison against [XX] peer organizations

### Scoring Methodology
- **0-25 (Initial)**: Ad hoc, no systematic approach
- **26-50 (Developing)**: Structure emerging, gaps in execution
- **51-75 (Defined)**: Systematic processes, scaling challenges
- **76-90 (Managed)**: Mature practices, continuous improvement
- **91-100 (Optimizing)**: Industry leadership
