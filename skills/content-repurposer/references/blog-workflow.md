## Step 5: Quote Graphics

### Selection Criteria

Best quotes from blog posts are:
- Self-contained (make sense without context)
- 1-3 sentences (under 40 words)
- Memorable or counterintuitive
- Actionable or insightful
- Shareable (people want to repost)

### Quote Collection

```markdown
# Quotes from: [Blog Title]

## Quote 1
> "[Quote text]"

- Section: [Where in blog]
- Platform: [Best fit]
- Graphic style: [Suggested design]
- Status: [ ] Created [ ] Posted

## Quote 2
> "[Quote text]"

[Same structure...]

## Quote 3
[Continue...]
```

### Quote Graphic Specs

| Platform | Dimensions | Text Size | Max Words |
|----------|------------|-----------|-----------|
| Instagram | 1080x1080 | 40-60pt | 30-40 |
| LinkedIn | 1200x1200 | 40-60pt | 30-40 |
| Twitter | 1600x900 | 36-48pt | 30-40 |
| Pinterest | 1000x1500 | 48-72pt | 20-30 |

## Step 10: Evergreen Social Rotation

### Building a Content Library

From each blog post, create:

```markdown
# Evergreen Content: [Blog Title]

## Tweets (10-15)
1. "[Quote or insight]" - [link]
2. "[Different angle]" - [link]
3. "[Question to engage]" - [link]
[Continue...]

## LinkedIn Posts (3-5)
1. [Topic angle 1] - short version
2. [Topic angle 2] - short version
3. [Story angle] - short version

## Instagram
- Carousel (reusable)
- 5x quote graphics
- 3x tip graphics

## Repromote Schedule
- Week 1: Original promotion
- Week 2: Different angle
- Month 2: Quote focus
- Month 3: Reshare
- Quarterly: Evergreen rotation
```

### Rotation Calendar

```
Month 1: Fresh content
- Original promotion across all channels
- Multiple angles first 2 weeks

Month 2-3: Secondary promotion
- Quote graphics
- Thread reshares (Twitter)
- Different hooks

Quarterly: Evergreen rotation
- Add to content rotation calendar
- Pair with timely hooks
- Update if needed
```
